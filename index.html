5. ADHD Consistency Hacks

The "10-Minute Lie": Tell yourself you will only study for 10 minutes. ADHD brains hate "starting" more than the work itself.
Body Doubling: Search "Study With Me" on YouTube to trick your brain into staying on task.
Brown Noise: Use "Deep Brown Noise" to muffle internal distracting thoughts and create a focus tunnel.
Novelty Reset: Switch rooms or sit on the floor when a subject feels stale. Change resets focus.

6. The Dopamine Break Menu

Avoid your phone during breaks. Use these 10-minute Active Breaks instead:

  • Physical: 15 pushups or 1-song dance.
  • Sensory: Splash ice-cold water on face.
  • Brain Reset: Step outside (no music).
  • Reward: High-protein snack.
